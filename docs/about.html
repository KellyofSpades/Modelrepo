Kelly Anderson

Hello! My name is Kelly Anderson, a second year student attending Penn State Behrend majoring in Digital Media and earning a certificate in German Studies.

My hobbies include...

  • Drawing
  • Painting
  • Playing Video Games

Ever since I was a kid, I've had a strong passion for art, mainly drawing over painting. Starting in 3rd grade I began to take art lessons and I took them until High School. I've been doing traditional art for over 11 years and digital art for 6 years. Most of my drawings are inspirations from video games I've played, cartoons I've watched, or other artist's work I've seen. My artstlye has always been a cartoony kind of style, and as of now, the style I draw in the most is Rubberhose. I've picked up more of this style from my favorite video game, Cuphead.

During High School, I was a javelin thrower and absolutely loved it. However, my javelin throwing career would soon come to a halt after a semester of college. In 2022, I've been slowly getting symptoms of Tendonitis in my right wrist and in 2023, the condition became more severe and bilateral. Javelin was stopped after my elbow had been affected as well. In my dominant arm, I had surgery for a Cubital Tunnel Decompression. The problem of Tendonitis still lingers as of early 2025.
